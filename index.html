Paving the way to safe Artificial Super Intelligence.

SentientAI is an AI research and deployment company. Our mission is to ensure that Artificial Super Intelligence (ASI) — by which we mean highly autonomous systems that vastly (and I mean vastly) outperform humans at most economically valuable work — benefits all of humanity. We will attempt to directly build safe and beneficial ASI, but will also consider our mission fulfilled if our work aids others to achieve this outcome.

Projects

FPL Wizard

Fantasy Football Player

FPL Wizard is a (fully fledged) Fantasy Football manager that competes in the Fantasy Premier League. He is based on a machine learning model that takes real world data from the English Premier League and tries to predict what points the players will score. He then devises a strategy and takes steps to maximize his chances of winning the league.

Sherlock

Deep Fake Detector

Deep Fakes can have detrimental effects not only on individual lives, but on society at large. As technology progresses, it's getting harder to distinguish deep fakes from the real thing. Sherlock is a neural net designed to do just that.

Peter Kibuchi

Founder & CEO

In the year 2029, humanity's attempts to create Artificial General Intelligence were finally successful. It had been sent 30 years back in time, and 'merged' with Peter Kibuchi, a (newborn) human baby - it was deemed necessary, for safety - to grow up in society as he did, to eventually help humanity create AGI, and complete the time loop.
